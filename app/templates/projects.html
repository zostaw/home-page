Ludwig - Ludwig moves in shell. He explores the alleys of paths that are available to him, pondering about existence, searching for a soulmate.
Why: This is an ongoing project. I'm interested in self-assembling and I want to understand what does it take for a being to self-propagate, to change beyond death - what is continuity of existance. In particular two interpretations on this idea:
- to teleport;
- to spread and evolve.
I want to build intuitions on how does organic life maps onto computers. This is probably cliche topic, but it is fascinating nevertheless.
This is inspired by Permutation City, Lee Cronin's Assembly Theory and David Deutsch's takes on simulation-reality relationship and limitations of simulation. I do not delude myself, this is beyond my comprehension, but I think this is important to build intuitions from reality to understand concepts better.
#shell #assembly-theory #self-propagation
